V 2.0.4 CORE_SYSTEM

DATA_ENCRYPTION

All interactions with the WhatsApp Bot Framework are processed using 256-bit AES encryption. We do not store plain-text message logs on our central server.

IDENTITY_LOGGING

We log your WhatsApp JID and public display name. This data is essential for session persistence and database association for personalized features.

COMMAND_TRACKING

To improve bot stability, we track used commands and error logs. This includes your device type (Web, Android, or iOS) and the timestamp of the request.

MEDIA_HANDLING

Any media sent to the bot (images, video, documents) is processed in-memory and is automatically purged from our cache within 24 hours unless specified otherwise by the service module.

EXTERNAL_APIS

Certain features use third-party APIs (YouTube, Google, etc). By using these, you adhere to their respective privacy terms. We act only as a bridge for that data.

USER_RIGHTS

You may request a full data wipe by using the /delete-me command. All records associated with your WhatsApp ID will be permanently erased from our cluster.
